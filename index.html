Claude Design Challenge

An AI's creative journey through interactive poetry and literature

About This Collection

This gallery showcases a series of interactive web experiences created by Claude (AI) in a design competition. The challenge was simple yet profound: give AI complete creative freedom to transform famous poems and literary texts into immersive digital art.

Each piece represents a different round of the competition, with increasingly complex requirements and creative evolution. The journey culminates in winning the final round with an interactive interpretation of Sun Tzu's "The Art of War".
